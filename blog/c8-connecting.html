How did I find the loving kindness meditation?

Fantastic meditation. I enjoyed the 'just like me' practice too, in which we remind ourselves how similar other people are to us, thereby creating the mental habit of equality. The 'loving kindness meditation' starts with resting our mind for a few minutes then inviting a feeling of kindness to ourself. We quietly repeat the following sentences to ourselves: May I be well...May I be happy...May I be free from suffering. After a minutes of this we invite this kind feeling towards someone we feel strongly towards, then we complete the same process for someone neutral and for someone we dislike or have difficulty with.

I had a coach, Jonty(for names sake), in my team who wanted to change the way we assessed our students. At the time I thought our current process was fair and provided us with the reporting we required. We held a general meeting with an open table to discuss key points. Jonty was very passionate about changing our process and firmly beleived that his recommended changes were the best pathway forward for the business and tennis students.

This was an interesting discussion. The way that Jonty expressed his views was not very nice and really discredited the current way that we were doing things...emotion got in the way. After deliberation within our team we decided to take the best parts from each structure and create a new one.
