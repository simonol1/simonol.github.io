How did I find the process over product concept?

I had to keep saying to myself "process over product". I felt intimidated at the beginning of the week when a few of my fellow cohort posted amazing websites. I tried to take inspiration from them, but starting focusing too much on the product and quickly become very frustated by my inability to firstly design a site them implement. I spent alot of time on the product and not the process this week...steep learning curve.

What did I think of Meditation before reading the 2nd chapter of 'search within yourself'?

These days everyone is in a hurry to get somewhere. People rarely stop and focus on the present moment without judgement. I frequently fall into this sinking ship. I think meditation is an amazing way to slow down and gain control of your mind. I try to practice on a daily basis when I have enough self control.. My understanding of meditation is: Mental training to improve attention awareness.

What new things have I learnt about meditation?

Happiness need not be pursued, but allowed. It is a default state of mind and meditation calms the mind to allow. I like the analogy of the gym and weight lifting. Everytime you lift a weight, the resistance increases muscle strength. Everytime your mind wanders and you bring it back to present you are building 'mental muscle' and improving your attention to the present moment.

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out for me?

Yeah - I like the process model which features the question before you start - what is my intention here? Why am I meditating? I also found the attitude concept good, adopting the 'grandmother mind' when talking to youself.

Any other musings?

The pursuit of happiness is interesting as are the studies conducted which show improvements in anxiety levels and immune system strength after 8 weeks of consistent meditation.
