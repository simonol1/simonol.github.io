Self Confidence

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self-awareness

Being mindful is also being self-aware. I found this chapter really interesting, particularly the part about self awareness to self confidence...deep self-knowledge and blatant self-honestly. In other words, knowing your strengths and weaknesses really well will build your self confidence.

I tried a practice called 'body scanning'...how did I find it?

I found this really good. It wasd helpful to have something to focus my attention on while I was meditating. When I reached my feet, I felt like I was in a trance. I'm planning to start using this practice while I'm lying in bed trying to go to sleep.

What did I take away from the 'My Emotions not me' section?

Interesting article. I've been thinking about how I experience my emotions at the moment and I will often say "I" before the emotion. The insight to say 'I'm experiencing anger' sounds like something a sociopath might say with total detachment from emotions, but maybe I've just watched too many movies. I see how this will be beneficial and I'll certainly work towards this amazing insight and self awareness.

Treat the ego with humour...let it be small enough that 'self' is not important and big enough that I'm comfortable in most situations.
